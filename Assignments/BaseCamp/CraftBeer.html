About 01000010_B

Founded by a rogue team of developers and brewmasters, Binary Brews is the result of a simple query: `SELECT beer FROM passion WHERE skill IN ('brewing', 'coding');`. We believe that the precision, creativity, and collaborative spirit of software development can be applied to create exceptional craft beers. We're constantly iterating, debugging, and deploying new flavors for you to enjoy. Welcome to our taproom.
